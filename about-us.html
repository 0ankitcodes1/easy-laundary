Our Story

Easy Laundry is a one-stop laundry and dry cleaning service in Nepal. Our main priority is to bring convenient and simple solutions for people’s dry cleaning and laundry needs especially in Kathmandu. We aim to make it easy for users to assign their cleaning to professionals and trust them with their clothes.
Even if it’s washing, pressing, dry cleaning, and folding, we ensure that your garments go through the best treatment. We aim to provide dry cleaning services to end-users and allow them to enjoy clean garments hassle-free. With our friendly website and mobile application, accessing the service is even easier.
We pick up your clothes from your doorstep and you can trust our group of experts and top-notch machinery to get you the cleanest laundry in time. Easy Laundry is not only punctual but lives up to the promises of the services we provide to customers. We want users to stop worrying about their clothes and garments that need to be cleaned and spend precious time at home with family or on vacation.

Our Vision

We aim to make the experience of always wearing the cleanest garments and clothes natural to our customers and just a touch away. We exist for you to enjoy clean garments and outfits hassle-free.

Our Mision

Our team of experts and sets of state-of-art machinery work to ensure our customer’s satisfaction. Additionally, we deploy user-friendly and convenient mobile applications and sites to ensure our service is accessible without any hassle.
